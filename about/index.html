About the book

YOU HAVE TOO MUCH SHIT is an angry little self-help book for people who have too much shit. Rather than offering gentle advice on how to de-clutter your life, YHTMS is designed to kick you squarely in the face. Buy the book or download it here.

About the author

Chris Thomas is a designer and writer with a passion for simplicity, everyday things and sneering cynicism. Educated at Goldsmiths in the depth of the recession, he now makes digital things for the nice people at Webcredible. View his personal website, or contact him at chris@thomaschris.co.uk.
